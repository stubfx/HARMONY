- **Tero, Atsushi et al. (2010)** — verificare la citazione esatta: «The Physarum network shows characteristics of good transport network design, namely high efficiency, low cost, and resilience against failures.» Fonte: *Rules for Biologically Inspired Adaptive Network Design*, Science, vol. 327, n. 5964, pp. 439–442. [capitolo2.md, sezione 2.1]
